# Permitting & Licensing — ROI Analysis (illustrative; customer-tunable)

> A worksheet, not a guarantee. Plug in the customer's volume and loaded labor rate. Comparable public results across the suite: Anne Arundel County cut a 45-minute task to under 20 seconds; Grupo TX reported ~35% call-volume reduction (AWS, 2026).

**Value levers:** permit cycle time, reviewer administration, resubmission rate, inspection scheduling.

Pair with `offerings/`-style TCO/ROI models for the CIO/CFO narrative. The hard-to-monetize gains — equitable access, accessibility, staff capacity redeployed to complex work, institutional-knowledge retention — are real and worth naming.
